## Discussion
in [[atomic-notes/Pre-requisites of Piagetian learning environments are people who live at the intersection of domain expertise, empathy, creativity, and communication.\|Pre-requisites of Piagetian learning environments are people who live at the intersection of domain expertise, empathy, creativity, and communication.]], Papert claims that there's a need for a role of people to accomplish these learning environments but no space for people of this role. I believe that is no longer true, but the space is not big enough yet. There are edu-tainment creators, science communicators, game based learning groups, etc. These are still a small part of the community but I am hopeful it is growing.
