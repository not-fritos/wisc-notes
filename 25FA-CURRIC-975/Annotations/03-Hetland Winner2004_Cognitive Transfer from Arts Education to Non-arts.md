>3 areas for casual relationship between arts and non-arts
>- classroom drama and verbal achievement (ability to articulate and creativity)
>- music listening and spatial reasoning
>- music instruction and spatial reasoning
>
>testing drama:
>- enactment, plot (level of structure), leader (teacher involvement)
>- testing could be either direct (re-enactment) or new (transfer) scenes
>- embodiement helped with recall and creativity
>- structured plots had higher effects when comprehension and structure were outcomes
>- unstructured plots had better oral language development
>** probably why improve classes are important
>- "what's remarkable is not that drama's strongest effects are direct ones, but that drama has the power to foster skills that then transfer to new material"
>- drama increases achievement levels of poor students (low SES) b/c of lack of creative, dramatic play, social success
