> > Mathpower, one might say, becomes a way of life. The sense of power is not only associated with immediately applicable methods such as the use of angular measure of variables, but also with such concepts as "theorem" or "proof' or "heuristic" or "problem-solving method." In using these concepts, the child is developing ways to talk about mathematics.. And it is to this development of mathematical articulateness we now turn.
