[[Microworlds are about abstraction]]

> [!PDF|note] [[Mindstorms.pdf#page=141&selection=145,20,161,34&color=note|Mindstorms, p.141]]
> > The next chapter discusses how this idea can be developed by constructing many such "microworlds," each with its own set of assumptions and constraints. Children get to know what it is like to explore the properties of a chosen microworld undisturbed by extraneous questions. In doing so they learn to transfer habits of exploration from their personal lives to the formal domain of scientific theory construction.
