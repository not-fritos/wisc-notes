> > Our fundamental position is that the relationship between social and computational sciences is more than a practical problem. Our goal has been to develop a stance in which ethnomethodology and computer science play equally significant roles (rather than grafting one onto the other), and so our approach is radically novel for both disciplines. We use the term “technomethodology” to emphasise that it is something new, drawing from each side, but different from each.
